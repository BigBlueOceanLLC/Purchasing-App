üéØ How the Approval Logic Works

Automatic Approval Criteria:

  • Current quota status is "under" or "good"
  • Adding the shipment won't push any product over quota limits
  • PO number is generated immediately

Manual Approval Required When:

  • Quota status is already "near-max" or "over"
  • Adding the shipment would exceed quota limits
  • Shipment appears in "Pending Purchases" tab
